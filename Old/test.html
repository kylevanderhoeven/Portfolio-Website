Playground

This page is basically just a place to mess around with random ideas.









  Click on any of the buttons to change the style of my logo. Each style has its own unique effect when you "roll over" it with your mouse. If you're viewing this from a tablet or phone, just tap it!
























  Roll over (or tap on) the image!















  Click anywhere in this box to make me jump!


  This is the most elaborate example showcased in this section. It's made up of 13 different components that I created in Adobe Illustrator.

  • Hat
  • Head
  • Smile
  • Open Mouth
  • Body
  • Left Arm
  • Right Arm
  • Left Leg
  • Right Leg
  • Left Arm (Jump)
  • Right Arm (Jump)
  • Left Leg (Jump)
  • Right Leg (Jump)

  These components are combined to create the character. His eyes are programmed to blink at set intervals and upon clicking in the action box, the components of the character are either swapped or repositioned to animate his jump. His "ready state" is animated to give a "breathing" effect.
