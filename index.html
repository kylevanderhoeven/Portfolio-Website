About

I’m a web developer and graphic designer based in Vancouver, British Columbia. I’m very passionate about user experience and front-end development. I’m a big fan of websites that have personality. This means a clean, attractive design complimented by strong functionality. The three most important things that fuel my design process are creativity, functionality and coffee. I’ve always had an active imagination and I love to create.
